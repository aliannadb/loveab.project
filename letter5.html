Dear Saturn,

i know that even when i’m scared to death, flipping over on the deadliest scariest funfair ride or trying to get out of the medina, i know that all it takes is to look you in the eye and nothing else matters because with you- no matter where we are, what it is that we’re going through, i know i will be fine as long as you’re next to me. In those eyes of yours i find safety despite the chaos of the world.

- Love, A. B
